# Glossary

## Model Validation

Independent process to assess whether a model is conceptually sound, performs as expected and remains suitable for its intended use.

---

## Model Risk

The risk of adverse consequences arising from decisions based on incorrect or misused models.

---

## Conceptual Soundness

Assessment of model design, assumptions, methodology and theoretical justification.

---

## Performance Validation

Evaluation of predictive performance using quantitative metrics.

---

## Stability

Assessment of model robustness over time and across different populations.

---

## Monitoring

Continuous evaluation of model performance after implementation.

---

## Governance

Policies, documentation and controls supporting the model lifecycle.
